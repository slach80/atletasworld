Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, date of birth (for age-group registration), and payment information. We also collect usage data such as session history and booking records.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your account and bookings
  • To send session reminders and confirmations
  • To process payments via Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • To improve our programs and communications

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • SendGrid / Mailgun — for transactional emails
  • Twilio — for SMS reminders (if opted in)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing us. Payment records are retained as required by law.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication and basic site functionality. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. Kansas residents may have additional rights under applicable state law.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: info@atletasperformancecenter.com

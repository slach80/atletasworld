Programs & Training

APC trains youth soccer players from U8 through U19, as well as high school and college-age athletes. The Serie A Elite Scouts class serves U13 (born 2014–2018) and U19 (born 2007–2013). The APC Select competitive program currently targets boys born 2014–2016. All skill levels are welcome.
APC offers private (1-on-1) sessions, semi-private (2–3 players), group sessions, team training, clinics, camps, pickup games, and seasonal packages. Special programs include:
  • → APC Select — elite supplemental competitive team (U10–U16)
  • → Serie A Elite Scouts — weekly Sunday class (U13 + U19)
  • → Summer Program — May through August, all ages
No prior experience is required for most programs. APC trains beginners through elite/competitive players and assigns skill levels — beginner, intermediate, advanced, and elite — to ensure appropriate placement. Private and semi-private sessions are especially well suited for players new to structured soccer training.
APC Select is a high-performance supplemental program — it does not replace your existing club commitment. Players train on one of three elite teams (born 2014, 2015, or 2016), competing monthly against ECNL, MLS Next, and international teams, with two indoor/outdoor sessions per month included at $100/month for a 6-month commitment.
A weekly 90-minute group session held every Sunday at APC, coached at a Serie A scouting standard. Two age groups:
  • → U13 class (born 2014–2018) — 4:00 PM
  • → U19 class (born 2007–2013) — 5:30 PM

Cost: $40 per session · No package required · Max 20 players per class. APC Select members receive a 10% discount.

Pricing & Packages

Pricing varies by program format:
  • → Serie A Elite Scouts class: $40 per session
  • → APC Select membership: $100/month (6-month commitment)
  • → Private & group sessions: priced per session or via package bundles

Training packages let you prepay for a set number of sessions at a discounted rate, valid for a specified number of weeks from purchase. Sessions are deducted from your balance each time you book. Packages are managed through the client portal — you can track remaining sessions and expiration dates at any time.
Yes. Each package has a validity period (number of weeks) stated at the time of purchase. Unused sessions expire at the end of that window. APC Select members receive an additional $40 monthly credit toward training packages plus a 10% discount on all camps and clinics.

Scheduling & Booking

  1. 1 Create a free account at atletasperformancecenter.com
  2. 2 Add your player profile (one account can have multiple players)
  3. 3 Browse available sessions, filter by type, coach, or time
  4. 4 Purchase a package or book a drop-in session
Cancellations are permitted more than 24 hours before the scheduled session start time, and the session credit is returned to your package balance. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session — and no-shows — may result in the session being forfeited. Check your booking in the client portal for the specific cancellation deadline.
Yes. One parent or guardian account can manage multiple player profiles under a single login. Each player can be enrolled in different programs or session types. Bookings are made per player, so each session is tracked individually in the client portal.

About Atletas World

Developing Champions On and Off the Field

Our Mission

At Atletas World, we believe every player has the potential to reach their highest level. Our mission is to provide world-class soccer training that develops not just athletic skills, but character, discipline, and a lifelong love for the game. Through personalized coaching and proven methodologies, we help athletes of all ages unlock their full potential.

Our Story

Founded by Coach Mirko Trapletti, Atletas World was born from a passion for developing young soccer talent. With over 10 years of international coaching experience across youth academies, college programs, and professional clubs, Mirko saw an opportunity to bring elite-level training to players of all skill levels.

What started as individual training sessions focused on tactics and conditioning has grown into a comprehensive training program featuring multiple coaches, diverse programs, and hundreds of satisfied families.

Today, Atletas World continues to expand, partnering with former professional players like Roger Espinoza to bring unparalleled expertise to our training programs.

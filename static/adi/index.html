About ADI

A New Model for Elite Athletes

ADI is an exciting new opportunity through Atletas World — an alternative to the traditional “sit in a classroom for hours” model. Students take control of their education while training and moving during the day, building their athletic identity alongside their academic one.

We partner with a fully accredited academic institution to combine high-level soccer training with structured academics — giving athletes the freedom to maximize potential on the field and flexibility to focus on the subjects they care about most.

Hosted at Avila University, Kansas City

# APC Select — Member Guide

## Your team is on the portal

When you log in, your dashboard now shows your Select team (2014, 2015, or 2016) along with your membership details.

---

## Practice sessions

You get **2 free practices per month** covered by your Select membership — no charge, no package sessions used. Your dashboard shows a live counter so you always know where you stand (e.g., "1 of 2 practices used this month").

If you want to come a **3rd time** in the same month, you can use a $40 monthly credit or a regular training package at checkout — your choice.

---

## Games

Games are scheduled by your coach and are **completely free** — they never count against your practices or credits.

When a game is posted, you'll get a **notification on your dashboard** with the date, time, and location. You just click **Going** or **Can't Go** right there — no emails to dig through, no forms to fill out.

---

## Credits

Your **$40/month credits** work exactly as before — apply them at checkout toward any APC training package or session.

---

## Membership renewal

Depending on how you enrolled:

- **Auto-renewal** — your membership renews automatically on the billing date. You'll get a heads-up 7 days before each renewal, a confirmation when it processes, and an alert if a payment ever fails so you can update your card before access lapses.
- **Manual renewal** — you'll get a reminder 30 days out and again 7 days before your expiry so you have time to renew at your own pace via the Packages page.

If you're on auto-renewal and want to cancel, go to **My Packages → Cancel auto-renewal**. Your access continues until the end of the period you already paid for.

---

## Where to find everything

| What | Where |
|---|---|
| Practice counter & credit balance | Dashboard → APC Select card |
| Upcoming games + RSVP | Dashboard → Upcoming Select Games |
| Renew / cancel subscription | Portal → Packages |

# SEO Audit — Atletas Performance Center
**Date:** May 16, 2026  
**Site:** atletasperformancecenter.com  
**Overall Score:** ~28–35/100

---

## Executive Summary

APC has excellent programs and a clean website, but is leaving significant organic traffic on the table due to missing local SEO infrastructure, no Google Business Profile, and a lack of content depth. Competitors like OP Soccer Club and KC Elite Soccer have stronger digital presence despite weaker programs.

**Estimated impact of fixing all gaps: $76,000+ in incremental annual revenue from organic traffic alone.**

---

## 🔴 Critical — Fix This Week

### 1. No Google Business Profile
APC has zero presence in Google Maps and the Local Pack. For searches like "youth soccer Overland Park" or "soccer training near me," APC does not appear at all. Competitors do.

**Fix:** Create a profile at business.google.com. Add full address (11301 W 88th St, Overland Park KS 66214), phone number, business hours, photos, and service area (Kansas City metro). This single action could triple local search visibility.

---

### 2. No sitemap.xml or robots.txt
Both `/sitemap.xml` and `/robots.txt` return 404 errors. Google cannot efficiently crawl or prioritize the site's pages.

**Fix:** Create both files. Add a `Sitemap:` directive in robots.txt pointing to the sitemap. Django has a built-in sitemaps framework — approximately 1 hour of development work.

---

### 3. Missing meta descriptions on /programs/ and /book/
The homepage has a good meta description. The Programs and Book pages have none, so Google writes its own — typically poor quality and low click-through rate.

**Suggested copy:**

*Programs page:*
> Explore APC Select elite teams, Serie A Elite Scouts, summer camps, and FIFA tournaments for youth soccer development in Overland Park, Kansas City.

*Book page:*
> Book elite youth soccer training sessions at Atletas Performance Center in Overland Park, KS. Private sessions, group training, and performance programs available.

---

### 4. No structured data (JSON-LD schema)
Google has no machine-readable signal that this is a local sports business. No LocalBusiness, SportsOrganization, or Event schema exists anywhere on the site. Competitors with schema markup receive rich results in Google Search.

**Fix:** Add a LocalBusiness + SportsActivityLocation JSON-LD block to the site's base template. This communicates the business name, address, phone, service area, and social profiles directly to Google.

---

### 5. No phone number on the website
Only an email address appears in the footer. A visible phone number is a core local SEO signal (NAP: Name, Address, Phone) and parents expect it before enrolling.

---

### 6. No testimonials or social proof
APC has completed 1,800+ sessions with a 5.0 rating — but none of this appears as actual quotes or reviews on the website. This is the #1 conversion blocker for youth sports businesses. Competitors show 30–50 reviews prominently.

**Fix:** Collect 5–10 quotes from existing clients and add a testimonials section to the homepage. A dedicated reviews page should follow.

### 8. H1 tag not keyword-optimized
**Current:** "Welcome to Atletas Performance Center"  
**Recommended:** "Elite Youth Soccer Training in Overland Park, Kansas City"

The brand name can appear as a subheading. The H1 should lead with what parents are searching for.

---

### 9. No Open Graph tags on inner pages
When the Programs or Book pages are shared on Facebook, text messages, or other platforms, they display no image or preview. The homepage has these tags; the inner pages do not.

---

### 10. No canonical tags
No pages declare a canonical URL, which creates risk of duplicate content penalties if URLs are accessed with/without trailing slashes or query parameters.

---

### 11. Tailwind CSS loaded from CDN (render-blocking)
The site loads Tailwind's full 70KB JavaScript file synchronously in the `<head>`, blocking all page rendering until it completes. This directly impacts Google's Core Web Vitals score:

- Estimated current PageSpeed score: **50–60**
- Estimated score after fix: **75–85+**
- Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) is likely 3–4 seconds; Google's target is under 2.5 seconds

**Long-term fix:** Build Tailwind into a static, minified CSS file using PurgeCSS (reduces to ~10–15KB). This is a development task of a few hours.  
**Short-term fix:** Add a `defer` attribute to the Tailwind script tag to reduce blocking impact immediately.

---

### 12. Images missing lazy loading and dimensions
No images on the site use `loading="lazy"` and none have explicit `width`/`height` attributes. Missing dimensions cause Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) — a Core Web Vitals penalty that affects search ranking.

**Fix:** Add `loading="lazy"` to all below-the-fold images. Add explicit dimensions to all `<img>` tags.

# APC Select — Monthly Auto-Pay Feature

**For Mirko's review before development begins.**

---

## What This Does

Instead of parents paying the full APC Select Membership price upfront, they can enroll in **monthly auto-pay**:

- Purchase the first month by **June 15th**
- Automatically charged **$100 on the 15th of each month** from July onward
- The **12 sessions are valid for the entire membership term** — they don't reset each month; parents use them across the full season

---

## How It Works for Parents

1. Parent logs into their APC account at `atletasperformancecenter.com`
2. Goes to **Packages** → sees the APC Select Membership card
3. Clicks **"Enroll in Auto-Pay"**
4. Reads and agrees to the auto-pay disclaimer (required checkbox)
5. Enters their card number — first payment of $100 is charged immediately
6. From then on, **$100 is automatically charged on the 15th of each month**
7. Sessions remain available throughout the term until all 12 are used

---

## Auto-Pay Disclaimer (what parents will see and agree to)

> **Auto-Pay Agreement**
>
> By enrolling in APC Select Auto-Pay, you authorize Atletas Performance Center to charge your card **$100 on the 15th of each month** for the duration of the membership term.
>
> **Cancellations must be requested directly from APC staff — you cannot cancel this from your account.** Contact info@atletasperformancecenter.com to cancel.

Parent must check a box before they can submit.

---

## How It Works for APC (Owner Side)

- The owner portal shows which clients have auto-pay active on their Select package
- To cancel a client's auto-pay, the owner clicks **"Cancel Auto-Pay"** on the client's profile — this stops future charges immediately
- When a payment processes on the 15th, the client's membership expiry automatically extends by one month
- Sessions are NOT reset on renewal — they carry over for the full term
